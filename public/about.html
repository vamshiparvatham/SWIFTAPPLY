About SwiftApply

SwiftApply is an innovative tool designed to automate the job and internship application process, simplifying the steps involved in applying to multiple platforms. Built using the Puppeteer library, SwiftApply navigates job boards, fills out application forms, and submits applications on behalf of the user. It saves time, enhances productivity, and makes it easy to apply for internships in just a few clicks.
